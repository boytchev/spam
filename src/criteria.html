Criteria

When some email makes me feel abused, forced or cheated, its metadata are added to this project. It is possible that a legitimate event or publication is wrongly included. If I cannot make myself sure within a minute that the message is trustful, it is spam. In a nutshell:

No tracking —

  • No email lists even if there is "unsubscribe" option
  • No mass email service before we have established contact
  • No trackers in links, images and buttons

No concealing —

  • No fake email addresses, domains or IP addresses
  • No links hidden behind other texts or images
  • No texts as images, except for logos

No begging —

  • No asking to write or to review a paper
  • No invitation to a conference or to a program committee
  • No offers for editing or publishing services, even for free

No impersonating —

  • No mimicking other sites or faking IF/SJR metrics
  • No sending email from public emails services
  • No emails about unknown events or journals, without proper contact
